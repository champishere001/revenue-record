Schedule 0.0 Block 5
List of Households & Selection
Questions to ask during listing
🏠 Household Listing Questions
Definition of Household: A group of persons normally living together and taking food from a common kitchen. Includes temporary stay-away but excludes temporary visitors.
For Each Dwelling Unit
"How many households are living in this dwelling unit?"
Record separate households if they have separate kitchens
Column (3) - Name of Head
"What is the name of the head of this household?"
Record full name as told by respondent
Column (4) - Household Size
"How many persons normally live in this household?"
Number of persons living and eating together
Column (5) - Second Stage Stratum (SSS)
Determine based on household characteristics:
1 Self-employed in agriculture
2 Self-employed in non-agriculture
3 Regular wage/salary earning
4 Casual labour (Urban only)
Column (6) - Education Level
"How many members in this household have passed secondary (10th standard) or above?"
Count of members with secondary education or higher
🎯 Selection of Sample Households
Selection Method: Systematic Random Sampling (SRSWOR) from each Second Stage Stratum (SSS)
Rural Areas
Select households from each SSS (1, 2, 3)
Total 8 households per FSU (distributed among SSS)
Urban Areas
Select households from each SSS (1, 2, 3, 4)
Total 8 households per FSU (distributed among SSS)

Schedule 10.4 Block 6
Current Weekly Activity Particulars
Reference Week: Last 7 days preceding survey
📅 Reference Week Structure
Reference Period: The 7 days ending on the day before the survey date. For each day, record:
  • Activity status (code)
  • Hours actually worked
  • Industry (NIC-2025 2-digit)
  • Wage earnings (for casual labour)
Day 1 (1st day): Most recent day (day before survey)
Day 7 (7th day): Earliest day (7 days before survey)
📝 Daily Activity Questions
For Each Day (Items 3.1 to 3.7)
Ask for each household member:
"Did [Name] do any work yesterday/the day before for pay, profit or family gain?"

If YES: "How many hours did [Name] work?"
If NO: "Why didn't [Name] work? Was it due to sickness, other reasons, or was there no work available?"
Column (4) - Status Code
Activity status for the day
11-51 Working (various categories)
61-62 Had HH work but didn't work
71-72 Had regular job but didn't work
81 Sought work
82 Didn't seek but available
91-97 Out of labour force
98 Temp sickness (casual only)
Column (5) - Industry
2-digit NIC-2025 code
Example: 84 (Public Administration)
Column (6) - Hours Worked
Hours actually worked (status 11-51) or would have worked (61,62,71,72)
Example: 8 hours
Column (7) - Total Hours
Total hours considering all work performed
Sum of all working hours
Column (8) - Hours Available
"If available for additional work, how many hours could [Name] have worked?"
Example: 0 (if not available)
Column (9) - Wage Earnings
For codes 41, 42, 51: Daily wage earnings (Rs.)
Record amount received/receivable
📊 Weekly Summary (Items 4-10)
Item 4 - Total Hours
Total hours actually worked during the week / hours available for additional work
Auto-sum of Items 3.1 to 3.7
Item 5 - Current Weekly Status
Status based on majority time or priority rules
Single code representing the week
Items 6-8
Industry, Occupation, Description
Based on CWS
Items 9-10
Earnings: Monthly (codes 31,71,72) or 30-day (codes 11,12,61,62)
Amount in Rs.
