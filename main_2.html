Schedule 0.0PL – Summary

Core Purpose: Schedule 0.0PL is the foundation of PLFS sampling – ensuring scientific selection of representative households

Key Takeaways

  • Foundational Document: All subsequent survey work depends on accurate 0.0PL listing
  • Timing: Fresh listing ONLY for new panels (first month of rotation)
  • Stratification Critical: SSS based on secondary+ education ensures educated households are adequately represented
  • Sample Size: Exactly 12 households per FSU (4+6+2 rural, 4+3+3+2 urban)
  • Quality Focus: Verify education levels, probe locked households, document all changes

Pre-Transition Checklist (Before Schedule 10.4)

  • All 12 households selected and verified?
  • SSS classification double-checked?
  • Substitutions documented with reasons?
  • Field operations block complete with signatures?
  • Remarks column filled for anomalies?
  • Office scrutiny completed?
Transition: Once 0.0PL is complete and scrutinized, proceed to Schedule 10.4 First Visit for detailed employment data collection

Schedule 10.4 – Overview

Employment & Unemployment Schedule

Primary instrument for collecting detailed labour force data from selected households

First Visit (Month 1)

  • Comprehensive Baseline: Full household roster
  • Usual Status: 365-day reference period
  • Current Weekly: 7-day activity details
  • Vocational Training: Detailed capture
  • Demographics: Complete education/attendance

Revisits (Months 2-4)

  • Change Tracking: Membership updates only
  • Current Weekly: Updated 7-day status
  • No Repeat: Usual status not re-collected
  • Efficient: Shorter questionnaire
  • Focus: Transitions and flows
Coverage: All members of 12 selected households per FSU | Frequency: 4 visits over 4 consecutive months per household

Household Characteristics (Full Collection)

Schedule 10.4 First Visit – Blocks 5.1 & 5.2

Usual Activity Status (365 Days Reference)

Block 5.1: Principal Activity

  • Status Code: 11-51 (Working), 81-97 (Not working)
    • 11=Worked in hh enterprise
    • 21=Worked as helper
    • 31=Regular salaried
    • 41=Casual wage public works
    • 51=Casual wage other
    • 81=Unemployed
    • 91-97=Not in labour force
  • Industry (NIC 5-digit): Detailed activity code
  • Occupation (NCO 3-digit): Type of work
  • Subsidiary Flag: Yes/No
  • Location: Home/away
  • Enterprise Type: Proprietary, Partnership, etc.
  • Contract Type: Written/oral/none
  • Social Security: PF, pension, etc.

Block 5.2: Subsidiary Activity

  • Condition: Only if "Yes" in Block 5.1 subsidiary flag
  • Definition: Secondary activity fetching ≥20% of total time
  • Same Codes: Status, Industry, Occupation
  • Time Spent: Hours/days in subsidiary work
Reference Period: 365 days preceding survey date (usual status)
Principal Activity Determination: Activity in which person spent major time during the 365 days. For equal time, use current weekly status as tie-breaker.
